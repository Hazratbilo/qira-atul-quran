Tajweed Classes

Tajweed is the set of rules that governs the proper pronunciation, articulation, and recitation of the Quran. In Qira’atul Qur’an services, Tajweed classes play a vital role in ensuring that students recite the Quran as it was revealed to Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ),Master the rules of Tajweed to enhance your Quranic recitation with accuracy and beauty.
