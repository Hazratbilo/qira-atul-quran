Qira'atul Qura'an School

Qira'atul Qur'an School is an educational institution dedicated to teaching the recitation of the Qur'an with proper Tajweed (pronunciation rules). The term "Qira'atul Qur'an" translates to "Recitation of the Qur'an," emphasizing the school's focus on accurate and melodious Qur'anic recitation.

Learn from experienced scholars and qaris to improve your Quranic recitation.

Enhancing Quranic recitation with precision and beauty.

Master the rules of tajweed to recite with accuracy and beauty.

Connect deeply with Allah and enhance your spiritual aspects in Islamic studies .
